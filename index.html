Integrated Tax Management

Real-time tax updates:
Tax Management Solution provides real-time updates on tax laws, regulations, and rates, ensuring that users are always compliant with the latest tax requirements.
Electronic Filing and Payment:
The system enables taxpayers to file their tax returns and make payments online, eliminating the need for manual submission of tax returns and payments at tax offices.
Compliance Monitoring:
The Tax Management Solution is able to monitor taxpayer compliance and send reminders to those who have not filed their tax returns or paid their taxes.

Biometric Voter Management

Biometric Data Collection:
The BVMS is able to collect biometric data such as fingerprints, facial recognition, or any other biometric data as required by the election commission.
Real-time Authentication:
The BVMS performs authentication of voters in real-time during the voting process, to ensure that only eligible voters are allowed to cast their votes.
Offline Functionality:
The BVMS is capabile of operating offline in case of network connectivity issues or power outages.
